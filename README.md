# Waste4Reward

## Product Case Study

Waste4Reward is a circular economy platform backed by The Coca-Cola Foundation, designed to incentivize, track, and monetize post-consumer plastic recovery through a digital marketplace and blockchain-enabled reward system.

## Problem

Large volumes of post-consumer plastic waste were moving through informal value chains without reliable recovery incentives, transparent tracking, or scalable monetization pathways. Collectors, aggregators, recyclers, and buyers needed a more structured and trusted system for plastic recovery.

## Product Solution

I led the development and go-to-market strategy for a digital marketplace and incentive layer using Plastoken Credit, blockchain infrastructure, and fintech-enabled payment workflows.

The platform connected collectors, aggregators, recyclers, and community participants while making plastic recovery traceable, incentivized, and commercially viable.

## My Role

As Product and Go-to-Market Lead, I designed the platform strategy, coordinated community recycling drives, onboarded aggregators and collectors, integrated recyclers into the supply pipeline, and supported the rollout of tokenized incentives for transparent and prompt payments.

## Key Product Features

* Collector onboarding
* Aggregator onboarding
* Recycler integration
* Digital marketplace for recovered plastic
* Tokenized incentive system
* Plastoken Credit reward layer
* Traceable payment workflows
* Community recycling campaign tools
* Recovery volume tracking
* Supply-chain visibility

## Product Impact

The platform supported the recovery of over 25,000 metric tons of plastic waste, generated over $3 million in recovered material value, attracted 1,000+ community members, and supported a community of 2,000+ entrepreneurs across collection and aggregation.

The project converted informal collection activity into a traceable supply chain, created new revenue streams for local entrepreneurs, improved buyer confidence, accelerated payment cycles, and demonstrated a scalable model for tokenized circular economy incentives.
